About Us

We are committed to providing quality services to patients at all times.

UniQare Medical Laboratories has been in existence for over 24 years under the name Arrow Medical Laboratory which was re-branded the year 2012 as UniQare Medical Laboratories with the aim of bringing change in the industry.


UniQare Laboratories is duly registered medical facility under Cap 499, Section 14 in the laws of Kenya.


UniQare Laboratories is a licensed institution with different branches across the country which are fully equipped with relevant Medical Equipment for diagnostics, highly qualified and experienced medical personnel from accredited Government Medical Training Institutions.

Our

Services

Medical Laboratory Tests

Carrying out all medical laboratory tests.

Consultation

Consultation on medical laboratory and diagnostic services including that of diagnosis of human ailments and components of the body and pathological investigations.

Food Handlers Tests

We carry out laboratory medical check up for food handlers within the region.

Preventive Health Services

Vaccination, immunization and dissemination of information on Typhoid, Meningitis, Hepatitis B, Cervical cancer and Influenza among others.

Drug Abuse Screen Testing (DAST) & Counseling

We carry out drug abuse testing, counseling, psychological evaluation, and psychiatric treatment support and consultation services.

Tailor-made Training Sessions

We offer we offer tailor-made trainings, campaigns and awareness on substance, alcohol and drug abuse use in corporates companies, schools, colleges and higher learning institutions.

Health & Diagnostic Education

Offering Health and Diagnostic Education (HDE) services and more so participate in current research activities, quality control programmes and health care services.

Medical Examination

We conduct medical examination for people who wish to travel outside the country for either job placements or those joining higher education colleges/universities. For the past years we have conducted medical examination for people travelling to Dubai, Qatar, Afghanistan, Kuwait, Lebanon, U.S, Germany, among others.
